Famous Attractions in Goa

Calangute Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its scenic beauty and lively atmosphere.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and a lighthouse.

Chapora Fort

Known for its panoramic views of the coastline, famously featured in Bollywood movies.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this church is famous for housing the relics of St. Francis Xavier.

Anjuna Beach

Known for its vibrant nightlife and stunning beach parties, Anjuna Beach is a popular destination in Goa.

Se Cathedral

A magnificent example of Portuguese colonial architecture, located in Old Goa.

Dona Paula

A scenic spot overlooking the Arabian Sea, offering panoramic views and a romantic atmosphere.

Miramar Beach

A beautiful and peaceful beach located near Panaji, ideal for leisurely strolls and relaxation.

South Goa

Known for its quiet, pristine beaches such as Palolem, and a more relaxed atmosphere compared to North Goa.

Cotigao Wildlife Sanctuary

A sanctuary offering a natural escape, home to a variety of wildlife species and rich flora.
