Famous Attractions in Kerala

Alleppey Backwaters

A picturesque waterway in Kerala, known for its houseboats.

Fort Kochi

A historic area known for its colonial architecture in Kochi.

Munnar Tea Gardens

Famous for its sprawling tea gardens and scenic landscapes in Munnar.

Varkala Beach

A beautiful beach in Kerala, known for its cliffs and serene atmosphere.

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Famous wildlife sanctuary in Thekkady, home to elephants and tigers.

Athirappilly Waterfalls

The largest waterfall in Kerala, located in Thrissur.

Kumarakom Backwaters

A tranquil waterway ideal for boat rides in the Kottayam district.

Silent Valley National Park

A pristine rainforest with rich biodiversity in Kerala.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A grand Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu in Thiruvananthapuram.
