Famous Attractions in Rajasthan

Amber Fort

A majestic hilltop fort with stunning architecture and panoramic views in Jaipur.

Hawa Mahal

A beautiful palace with 953 small windows in Jaipur, also known as the 'Palace of Winds.'

Jantar Mantar

An astronomical observatory in Jaipur with remarkable instruments to measure time and celestial bodies.

Udaipur City Palace

A grand palace complex overlooking Lake Pichola, famous for its stunning architecture and beautiful courtyards.

Mehrangarh Fort

A massive fort in Jodhpur offering incredible views and a rich history.

Pushkar Lake

A sacred lake in Pushkar, surrounded by over 50 temples and 500 shrines.

Jaisalmer Fort

A golden fort in the desert, also known as Sonar Quila, in the heart of Jaisalmer.

Ranthambore National Park

One of India's largest national parks, famous for its population of tigers and diverse wildlife.

Lake Pichola

A serene man-made lake in Udaipur, known for its boat rides and picturesque surroundings.

Fatehpur Sikri

A historical city near Agra, built by Emperor Akbar, featuring stunning Mughal architecture.
