Karnataka's Top 10 Attractions

Bangalore Palace

A beautiful palace built in Tudor style located in the heart of Bangalore.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its ruins of the Vijayanagar Empire.

Mysore Palace

A historic royal residence, showcasing beautiful architecture and art.

Coorg

Known for its coffee plantations, lush greenery, and beautiful landscapes.

Badami

Famous for its ancient rock-cut temples and historic significance.

Gokarna

A coastal town famous for its temples and pristine beaches.

Chikmagalur

A hill station known for its coffee estates and scenic beauty.

Kabini

Famous for its wildlife sanctuary and river views.

Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary

A popular bird sanctuary near Mysore, home to many migratory birds.

Nandi Hills

A popular hill station offering breathtaking views and a perfect sunrise spot.
